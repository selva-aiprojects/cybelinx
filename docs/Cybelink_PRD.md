# Cybelink Corporate Website PRD

**Version:** 1.0\
**Project Type:** Enterprise Product Engineering Website

## Vision

Build a world-class enterprise website positioning Cybelink as a Product
Engineering company delivering AI-powered digital products, cloud-native
platforms, and industry solutions across Banking & FinTech, Healthcare,
Pharma, HCM, Manufacturing, Retail, Logistics, Telecom, and Energy.

## Design Inspiration

The approved direction is a premium dark-themed experience with: -
Animated globe integrated with a glowing **C** brand mark - Enterprise
SaaS visual language - Glassmorphism - Blue, cyan, and violet
gradients - Premium typography - Smooth micro-interactions - Responsive
design - Modern product showcase

## Objectives

-   Generate enterprise leads
-   Showcase products and engineering capability
-   Demonstrate domain expertise
-   Support sales and partner conversations
-   Establish a premium global brand
